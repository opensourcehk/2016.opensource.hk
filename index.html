What is HKOSCon?

Hong Kong Open Source Conference is an annual international open source conference in Hong Kong. It is aimed to demonstrate the use of open source software technology, open hardware technology and latest development in the world.

Contact Organiser

Hong Kong Creative Open Technology Association (HKCOTA), Hong Kong Linux User Group (HKLUG) and Open Source Hong Kong (OSHK) are organisers of HKOSCon 2016.

HKOSCon 2016 is administrated by Hong Kong Creative Open Technology Association (HKCOTA) which is a non-profit tax-exempted charity organisation in Hong Kong.

For enquiry, please feel freely to email to us.
